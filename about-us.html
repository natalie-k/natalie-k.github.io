Who We Are


We’re a small startup based in Hawai’i, established in 2020. Our name, Choke Change, has multiple meanings. Choke means what most of us know it as, but it also means “a lot” in Pidgin. Change can mean "to make a difference," or it can mean "coins." We're aiming to earn “choke change” to donate to our main goals in order to bring “choke change.”
