Who We Are


We’re a small startup based in Hawaii, established in 2020. Our name, Choke Change, has multiple meanings. Choke means what most of us know it as, but it also means “a lot” in Pidgin. The word "change" can mean making a difference, or it could refer to money. So, you could say that we want to earn “choke change” to bring “choke change” through selling our merch and donating.

Our Goals

Goal 1: Affordable Housing

Hawaii is well-known for having the highest living costs in the U.S., and because of this, many of the residents struggle to afford housing.

Our business aims to fundraise enough to purchase Hawaiian land and create a niche housing design that not only blends with the land but is also relatively affordable.
