It's a well-known fact that Hawai'i has one of the highest living costs in the U.S.. Because of this, many of the state's residents struggle to afford housing. We want to change that.

Our business aims to fundraise enough to purchase Hawaiian land and create a niche housing design that not only blends with the land but is also relatively affordable.
